Brief Bio

I got a Ph.D degree from Institution of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) in 2009. During 2009 - 2011, I was a researcher at Samsung Advanced Institute of Technology (SAIT), China Lab. I went back to NLPR, CASIA as an assistant professor (2011-2015), and came to US as a research scholar at SUNY Albany (2013-2015, Supervisor: Prof. Siwei Lyu). I spent 3.5 years at Snap (2015 - 2019). In April 2019, I joined ByteDance as a software engineer.

My research interests include computer vision, computer graphics and image processing. I am particularly interested in porting useful algorithms to mobile devices with smart designs and optimizations.
